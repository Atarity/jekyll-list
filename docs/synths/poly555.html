POLY555

- 20 keys control 20 555 timers under a plexiglass window - Custom PCB and 3D-printed hardware, all designed by yours truly - Satisfying clicky tactile switches - Built-in amplifier and speaker - Soldered, assembled, tuned, and ready to go!
