Teensy Audio FX
Playable effects modeled on the Teenage Engineering PO series
'- A Teensy 4 based FX unit with Pocket Operator style and playability. - 12 knobs, 4 buttons each with LED, and 4 LEDs showing automation cycle. - Combination of filtered reverb, ping pong filtered delay and a noise source, but this is customisable. - Each knob can be automated (a bit like punch in effects on the Pocket Operators) - Tap tempo and PO sync
