x0x heart

Feb 17, 2025 • Open Music Labs

Heart of the infamous TB-303, surgically extracted to be transplanted into your designs.

The x0x-heart is just the analog section of the TB-303, replicated with SMT components, and made small enough to fit into a Eurorack modular format. It even has a Eurorack power header on it. More in OML wiki
