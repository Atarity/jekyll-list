Wee Noise Maker

Feb 17, 2025 • Fabien Chouteau

Pocket size sampler/sequencer with audio DAC, headphone amplifier and audio input.

Wee Noise Maker is a pocket size music synthesizer based on the STM32F427 ARM Cortex-M4F micro-controller. It features an audio DAC with headphone amplifier, audio input, 96x16 OLED screen, 2 rotary encoders, 24 tactile switches and LEDs, micro SD card slot, LiPo battery charger and an I2C extension port.
